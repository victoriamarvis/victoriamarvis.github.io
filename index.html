About Me

I am a Data Science & Analytics student with a strong interest in how technology can improve lives — especially in underserved communities. My work sits at the intersection of health-tech, UI/UX design, and data-driven decision making.

What drives me is the belief that well-designed systems can close gaps in access to information and care. From building reproductive health platforms for teenagers to hands-on networking and virtualisation labs, I enjoy working on projects that are both technically grounded and socially meaningful.
